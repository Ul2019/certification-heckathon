About The Web App

The SAPS Web App is an open platform for everyone to certify their documents online ( ID, birth certificates etc) without them having to go to the police station. Below are the examples of how the Website functions

  • If it's your first time to use the website then you have to register
  • For security purposes you have to use face recognition to verify your account
  • You then have to upload your files, using the upload button
  • Then allow the website to electronicaly certify your documents and one show recieve them in a PDF form
